M&A Advisory Services

Our M&A expertise comes from both the sell and buy side and working with both strategic and financial buyers.

• Representing sellers of closely-held companies
• Recapitalizing businesses
• Targeted Strategic Acquisition Searches
• Industry roll-ups
• Divestitures

Benefit from an advisor who has seen deals from all sides. Our understanding of both the seller’s and buyer’s perspectives makes us effective, zealous advocates of your interests. We use our operating experience to develop a true understanding of your business, its value and your goals.

Our M&A experience includes a highly successful roll-up of dozens of companies in the physical security industry; working with both strategic and financial buyers; divestitures of business units; sale and acquisitions of closely-held companies; representation of both buyers and sellers as an attorney in private practice; and extensive experience in due diligence.

We will support you throughout the life cycle of the deal: weighing strategic alternatives, preparing for sale, diligence, valuation, marketing the business, negotiating and closing the transaction.
